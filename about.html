About Me

I'm Oluwatobi, a UI/UX Designer. I design digital products and create beautiful experience for the users. Building intelligent products that can transform the society in a positive way gives me joy, I care so much about human problems and how to solve them. Beyond digital product design, I write front end codes too!

I have a background in Meteorology and Climate Science where I learnt a lot about the atmosphere, global warming, climate change and the threat they pose to the earth and it's inhabitants.

During my free time, I read about smart cities. I have hope that we can redesign cities across Africa for a sustainable future.

I'm looking forward to being planted in a community where my potential as a designer and front end developer is fully explored.
